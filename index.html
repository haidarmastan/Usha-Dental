Genuine Family Healthcare

We are well known for conservative and minimal invasive treatment. Our primary objective are:
1. Patient satisfaction.
2. Provide each patient best possible treatment based on individual needs.

Most importantly we have designed the practice in a such way that helps us to treat our in a calm and comfortable environment. We offer the best in Chhattisgarh, at our specialized fully equipped experienced of advanced dental care treatment under the hands of best specialized dentists. We start cautiously surveying your clinical and dental needs, by taking notes of all your dental worries and the problem that you might be encountering.

Usha dental provides dental solution ranging from root canal treatment , cosmetic treatment [best in c.g],dental braces, aligners (invisible braces),dental crown,dental bridges, pediatric treatment, laser dentistry, veneer and esthetic dentistry, flap surgery.

Specialized in state of the art treatment ,product and minimally invasive procedures aimed at improving appearance of the face. We are the best for creating smile without damaging your tooth in only single sitting. The right expertise and technology enable us to undertake advanced smile makeover, full mouth dental implants, zirconia and cadcam dental crown, laser dentistry and more. We use finest quality of materials and equipment imported from USA, Germany, Japan, Australia to keep pace with improvement in dental technology internationally. We maintain high standards of hygiene and adhere to sterilization protocols for your safety. Our responsibility towards you begin much before you sit on the dental chair.
